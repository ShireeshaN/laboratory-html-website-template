Why Infant & Child Nutrition is Important

Brain Development: Essential nutrients like DHA and iron are vital for brain growth and cognitive development.

Immune System: Proper nutrition strengthens the immune system, helping to protect against illnesses and infections.

Growth: Adequate intake of vitamins and minerals ensures proper physical growth and bone development.

Overall Health: Balanced nutrition promotes overall health and reduces the risk of chronic diseases later in life.

New Born Care: Busting Myths & Highlighting Facts:

Myth: Fat Should Be Avoided in a Child's Diet.

Fact: Healthy fats are essential for a child's brain development and overall growth. Foods like avocados, nut butters, and fish provide beneficial fats. It's important to include these in your child's diet while avoiding trans fats and excessive saturated fats.
